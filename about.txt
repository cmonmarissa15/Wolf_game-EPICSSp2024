Welcome to Wolf Adventure!
This game will have three chapters, each with their own special parts:


Chapter 1: You play as a baby wolf that will have to collect more food than 
his fellow cubs to be able to survive in the wild.


Chapter 2: You are now all grown up and need to go find a pack of your own!


Chapter 3: Once you find your pack, you have to keep your pack fed for one 
year. To do this, you will have to find the wolves from your pack spread 
across the forest to hunt down a bison.


Wolf Adventure was created in 2021 by Elizabeth Perkinson and Robert Rattray;
developed and updated by Karthik Nimmala, Armado Vargo, and Cole Hacker.


Press spacebar to exit.
