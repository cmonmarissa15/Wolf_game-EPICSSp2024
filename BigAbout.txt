Welcome to Wolf Park!!

To use the interactive map that gives you info and locations of the 
species of the park, use the "MAP" button.

To play the "Wolf Adventure" video game, use the "GAME" button.

To update sounds and character narration, use the "Settings" button.

Wolf Adventure was created in 2021 by Elizabeth Perkinson and Robert Rattray;
developed and updated by Karthik Nimmala, Armado Vargo, and Cole Hacker.

The Interactive Map was created by Sydney Loudermilk in 2023.

Please press "spacebar" to exit.
